Support BetaOnline!

Although Betaonline is primarily ran for fun as a side-project by the owner, it does cost money to maintain a Minecraft server. Your contributions help us cover the costs of hosting, upgrading hardware, maintaining the domain, and other expenses that keep the server online.

Unlike other servers, we do not have pay-to-win rewards for donations. The only reward you will receive is a cosmetic rank both in-game and on our Discord server. You also keep the server running, which is a reward in and of itself.

All donations, no matter the amount, are kindly received and are guaranteed to go to the maintenance and uptime of the server.
